⭐ IMPORTANT SAFETY GUARANTEE

To ensure no preset ever flickers dangerously on mobile, include this in your engine:

Temporal Smoothing Layer

Before rendering each frame:

field = lerp(prevField, newField, 0.15);

This reduces apparent motion speed by ~85%.

This single line prevents:
	•	rapid flashes
	•	chaotic full-screen brightness inversions
	•	temporal aliasing
	•	any seizure-risk patterns

⭐ Swipe Experience (Like Instagram Filters)

Each preset must feel like a style, not a system mode.

When user swipes:
	•	Transition regime parameters over 500ms (ease-in-out)
	•	Fade in a small label (e.g., “Fractal Pulse — Slow Mode”)
	•	Keep the field continuous (no hard reset unless needed)

Example transition:

animateParams(currentParams, targetPresetParams, 500);

Smooth, elegant, safe.
